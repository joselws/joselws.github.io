About

The fist working version of this project (as of this writing) was finished on Date numth 2022.

This is a Django project that places emphasis on managing user wallets. It's a helping tool to keep track of your personal finances by adding, substracting, and moving your money around.

Each wallet has a percent and a cap property. The percent property is how the money will distribute among the wallets when you make a deposit into your account. For example, if you have three wallets with 70%, 20%, and 10%, and you make an account deposit of $1000, the wallets will receive $700, $200, and $100 respectively. Of course, you can always choose to add money into a specific wallet independently.

The cap property represents the upper limit of how much money a wallet may hold. For example, you might not want a hypothetic savings account to surpass $60,000 and instead redistribute its part of the money on a deposit among the other wallets.

You may change the percent and cap properties of each wallet anytime you want.
